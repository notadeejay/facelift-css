Part 2

WBS Practice

A WBS is usually created collaboratively by the project team, not just the project manager. Why do you think this is?

Getting the whole team together means you get the experts involved; remember that a team is made up of people who have expertise in different fields. Having the team collaborate on the WBS can also be a constructive way for the team to come together at an early stage in the project. It also allows for the team to have oversight in all the deliverables of the project.

Creating the WBS as a team is usually done in a meeting and can take several hours or even a day. Often, the WBS is created over multiple sessions before it is considered complete and ready to be referenced by the team. To get a feel for what making a WBS looks like with a team, watch this video of a walkthrough of a project team making a WBS. In the example, Tom is a project manager for a project that involves making a smart music armband.
