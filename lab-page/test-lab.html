Getting Started with Google Sheets

Welcome to your first official Google Sheets lab!

In this lab, you will practice navigating Google Sheets. You will also learn how to enter data into a cell and navigate through cells in the sheet. All of the labs in this course will be in Google Sheets.

Before moving onto more complex labs, it is important to solidify your understanding of how to navigate Google Sheets and how to enter data to a spreadsheet.

Follow the instructions below. Remember, to enlarge images and GIFs throughout the lab, just click on them! When you are finished with the lab, click “Finish” at the bottom of the sidebar. 

Enter Data

In this lab, you will write the values from the table below into your spreadsheet.

Name  Bedtime Sleep Duration
Matt  12:00 AM 6.5 hours
Juan 8:00 PM 8 hours
 Rahul  11:00 PM  9 hours

Instructions to type the data into the spreadsheet:

1. Click on cell A1 to select it. A blue border will surround the selected cell.

2. Type the first piece of data into the cell. For A1, you will need to type Name.

3. Under the Name cell, continue to type in the remainder of the values within the first column.

4. Repeat steps 1, 2, and 3 on the next column to type the rest of the data and complete the table. Make sure you are incorporating the correct capitalization, spacing, and values for each cell.

ProTip

To quickly move down a cell, click “enter”, and to quickly move to the cell to the right, click “tab.”

To move up, down, right, and left press “tab” and the arrow keys simultaneously.

Congratulations! 🎉You've completed your first table in Google Sheets.
